# TROUBLESHOOT: Common Issues

## ChatGPT gives long responses
Add to your prompt: "Keep each response to 2-3 short paragraphs."

## ChatGPT doesn't give choices
Say: "At the end of your next response, give me exactly 2 choices for what to do. Number them 1 and 2."

## Story goes off track
Say: "Let's go back. Before I chose X, what would have happened if I chose Y instead?" Or: "Start a new story. Same idea, different setting."

## Not sure what to pick
Any choice works. The point is to see how the story changes. Pick randomly if you're stuck—it's for fun.
