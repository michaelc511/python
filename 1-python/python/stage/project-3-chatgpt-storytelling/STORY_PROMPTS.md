# ChatGPT-Based Storytelling
## Interactive Story Adventure - No Coding Required

Use ChatGPT to co-create an interactive story. Copy these prompts into ChatGPT and follow along.

---

## How It Works

You and ChatGPT take turns. You make choices. ChatGPT continues the story based on your choices. The story bends based on what you decide.

---

## Starter Prompt (Copy this first)

```
Let's create an interactive story together. You write a scene and give me 2-3 choices at the end. I'll pick one, and you continue the story from there. Keep each scene short (2-4 paragraphs). Start with a mysterious setting—maybe I wake up somewhere strange. Go!
```

---

## Example Choices You Might Make

- "I choose to go left."
- "I pick option 2."
- "I search the room first before leaving."
- "I ask the stranger who they are."

---

## Tips for a Great Story

1. **Be specific** – "I open the door slowly" works better than "I go."
2. **Ask for choices** – If ChatGPT doesn't give you options, say "Give me 2 choices for what to do next."
3. **Change direction** – Say "What if I had chosen the other option?" to explore alternate paths.
4. **Add twists** – "Surprise me with something unexpected" keeps it fun.
